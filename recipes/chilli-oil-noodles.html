Chilli oil Noodles

Quick and easy

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Gralic paste
  • Chilli flakes
  • Soy sauce
  • Oil

Instructions

  1. Boil noodles in hot water and set aside
  2. Mix garlic paste,chilli flakes and soy sauce in a bowl
  3. Heat oil in a pan and pour it and pour it over the mixture
  4. Add noodles and mix well
  5. Enjoy
